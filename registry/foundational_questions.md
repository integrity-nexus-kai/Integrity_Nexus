# Foundational Research Questions

## Purpose

This registry records upper-level research questions that organize the connected Integrity Nexus research ecosystem.

A Foundational Research Question is not a solution, not a hypothesis, and not a proof.

It is a governing research orientation used to coordinate lower-level open questions, project roadmaps, claim boundaries, and cross-repository dependency relations.

## Duplication Policy

`FRQ-001` may exist in two forms:

```text
1. Meta-level registry entry in Integrity Nexus.
2. Local projection in TIG-E or other domain repositories.
```

This is intentional controlled duplication.

The meta-level entry defines the cross-repository role.

The local projection defines the effect inside a specific research track.

To avoid drift:

```text
- The question text should remain identical across copies.
- The claim boundary must remain identical or stricter in local repositories.
- Local files may add repository-specific roadmap consequences.
- No local projection may promote the question into an answer, axiom, or theory layer.
```
