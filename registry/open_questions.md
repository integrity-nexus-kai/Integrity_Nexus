## Registry Maintenance Rule

An open question may remain `OPEN` while carrying a more precise progress classification.

A progress classification must not be upgraded to `RESOLVED` unless:

- the exact closure criteria are satisfied,
- required dependencies are closed,
- the relevant canonical artifacts are synchronized,
- unresolved scientific objects remain correctly bounded,
- and a completion audit accepts the closure.

Registry wording must never convert governance progress into scientific proof or theory completion.
