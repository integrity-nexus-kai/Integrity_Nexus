# SSC — Structural State Controller

## Role

SSC is the AI-control and state-admissibility research program within the Integrity Nexus ecosystem.

---

# Domain

```text
AI control / cyber-security / systems architecture
```

---

# Focus

SSC investigates bounded state evolution in control architectures.

Core themes include:

- supervisor-independent control,
- state admissibility,
- control invariants,
- verifier models,
- enforcement models,
- proof obligations,
- and capability boundary integrity.

---

# Relation to Integrity Nexus

SSC contributes the control-state projection of the shared denominator:

```text
Integrity-preserving bounded structural evolution
```

---

# Boundary

SSC should not be treated as a complete AI-alignment solution or a deployed AI-control system.

It is currently a governed research program for control-boundary specification.
