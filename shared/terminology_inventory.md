## 4. Inventory Classification Rules

Each term in this inventory must be read as one or more of the following:

- **Shared orientation term** — supports navigation but does not impose technical identity.
- **Repository-local technical term** — defined only within a stated repository and scope.
- **Claim-status term** — classifies evidential or governance status.
- **Maturity term** — classifies artifact readiness, not truth.
- **Named but undefined object** — may be referenced as open research target only.
- **Bridge term** — describes a required or existing interface; requirement and existence must remain distinct.

No term may be silently reclassified.

## 7. Maintenance Rule

A term must be added or updated here when:

- it appears materially in more than one repository;
- its local definition changes;
- its claim status changes;
- a bridge is created, rejected, or reclassified;
- a previous non-equivalence becomes an explicitly supported relation;
- or an audit identifies new terminology drift.

Updates must preserve exact repository paths and must not create definitions absent from the source repositories.
