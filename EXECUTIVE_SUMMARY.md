# Executive Summary

Integrity Nexus is the navigation and coherence layer for the integrity-oriented research ecosystem developed by Kai Stefan Dietrich.

It connects research repositories such as TIG, SIR, and SSC without replacing them or merging their claims.

---

# Core Function

Integrity Nexus exists to make the research ecosystem readable.

It provides:

- repository navigation,
- cross-repository conceptual mapping,
- shared terminology,
- dependency orientation,
- governance standards,
- and claim-boundary discipline.

---

# Shared Research Denominator

The common denominator across the connected repositories is:

```text
Integrity-preserving bounded structural evolution
```

Different repositories study this principle in different domains.

TIG applies it to bounded gravitational structure.

SIR applies it to recursive integrity systems.

SSC applies it to state-control and AI-control boundary architectures.

---

# Strategic Role

Integrity Nexus is not another research theory.

It is a meta-structure.

It makes the full research program navigable for:

- scientific reviewers,
- AI research teams,
- systems architects,
- governance auditors,
- and machine-assisted analysis pipelines.

---

# Current Status

Integrity Nexus is an early-stage meta-repository.

It currently provides the first public navigation layer and governance frame for the broader integrity research architecture.
