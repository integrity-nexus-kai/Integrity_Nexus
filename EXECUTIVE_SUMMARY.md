# Executive Summary

Integrity Nexus is the navigation, governance, and coherence layer for the integrity-oriented research ecosystem developed by Kai Stefan Dietrich.

It connects research repositories such as TIG, SIR, and SSC without replacing them, merging their claims, or turning conceptual relationships into proofs.

---

# Problem

A multi-repository research ecosystem can become difficult to review when concepts, claims, dependencies, and maturity levels are distributed across separate repositories.

Without a meta-structure, reviewers may not see:

- which repository owns which research object,
- how concepts relate across domains,
- which claims are supported,
- which claims remain open,
- and which governance rules prevent claim inflation.

---

# Observation

Across the connected repositories, a recurring structural pattern appears:

```text
systems evolve,
integrity must be preserved,
and admissible transitions require boundaries, constraints, or invariants.
```

This pattern appears in different domains without collapsing those domains into one theory.

---

# Approach

Integrity Nexus provides:

- repository navigation,
- cross-repository conceptual mapping,
- shared terminology,
- dependency orientation,
- maturity classification,
- governance standards,
- citation discipline,
- and claim-boundary control.

---

# Shared Research Denominator

The common denominator across the connected repositories is:

```text
Integrity-preserving bounded structural evolution
```

TIG applies this to bounded gravitational and geometric structure.

SIR applies this to recursive integrity and governance structures.

SSC applies this to state-control and AI-control boundary architectures.

---

# Repository Roles

## TIG

Mathematical-physics research program for bounded gravitational and geometric structure.

## SIR

Recursive-integrity and governance research program for constraint navigation and research-system coherence.

## SSC

AI-control and state-admissibility research program for supervisor-independent control boundaries.

# Current Status

Integrity Nexus is an early-stage public meta-repository.

It currently provides:

- navigation layer,
- common denominator definition,
- repository map,
- governance standards,
- maturity model,
- repository status index,
- shared concepts,
- and cross-repository dependency mapping.

---

# Open Questions

Current open meta-level questions include:

- How should cross-repository claims be classified?
- How can shared terminology remain useful without collapsing domain boundaries?
- What is the shortest review path for external scientific readers?
- How should the ecosystem become increasingly machine-readable for LLM-based research audit?

---

# Non-Claim

Integrity Nexus does not validate TIG, SIR, SSC, or any future repository by itself.

It maps, governs, and connects them.
