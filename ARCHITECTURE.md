# Architecture

Integrity Nexus is a meta-repository.

Its architecture is designed to connect research repositories without replacing their internal structures.

---

# Layer Model

```text
Integrity Nexus
  ↓
Repository Map
  ↓
Project Cards
  ↓
Shared Concepts
  ↓
Cross-Repository Dependencies
  ↓
Governance Standards
  ↓
Publication / Review Orientation
```

---

# Core Layers

## Navigation Layer

Files:

- `README.md`
- `EXECUTIVE_SUMMARY.md`
- `NAVIGATION.md`
- `REPOSITORY_MAP.md`

Purpose:

```text
Help readers understand where to go first.
```

---

## Coherence Layer

Files:

- `COMMON_DENOMINATOR.md`
- `registry/shared_concepts.md`
- `registry/cross_repo_dependencies.md`

Purpose:

```text
Explain how repositories relate without merging their domains.
```

---

## Project Layer

Files:

- `projects/TIG.md`
- `projects/SIR.md`
- `projects/SSC.md`

Purpose:

```text
Provide compact project cards for each connected repository.
```

---

## Governance Layer

Files:

- `governance/README.md`
- `governance/repository_standard.md`
- `governance/claim_boundary_standard.md`
- `governance/citation_standard.md`

Purpose:

```text
Maintain claim discipline and cross-repository consistency.
```

---

# Non-Goal

Integrity Nexus is not intended to become a replacement for TIG, SIR, SSC, or any future research repository.

It is a map, not the territory.
