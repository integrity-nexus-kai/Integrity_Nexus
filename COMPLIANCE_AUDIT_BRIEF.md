## Repository Function

Integrity Nexus is a meta-repository for:

- research governance,
- dependency mapping,
- cross-repository integrity architecture,
- operational research-program management,
- audit flow,
- publication coordination,
- strategy,
- operations,
- metrics,
- and object-centered research state management.

Integrity Nexus does not replace the underlying research repositories.

It does not validate or prove the domain-specific claims of TIG, SIR, SSC, or future connected repositories.

## Required Audit Scope

Audit Integrity Nexus for:

1. authorship clarity,
2. citation metadata,
3. license presence,
4. role clarity,
5. governance consistency,
6. claim-boundary discipline,
7. dependency classification,
8. publication-readiness discipline,
9. object-model consistency,
10. operational usability.

Do not audit whether TIG, SIR, or SSC are scientifically true.
