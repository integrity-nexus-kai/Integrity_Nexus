# Risk Register

## Purpose

This document records operational and research risks across Integrity Nexus and connected repositories.

---

# Active Risks

## RISK-001 — Structure Without Execution

Severity: high  
Status: active

Risk:

```text
The ecosystem may become highly structured without converting structure into active research progress.
```

Mitigation:

```text
Maintain weekly reviews, active roadmap items, and measurable success metrics.
```

---

## RISK-002 — Claim Inflation

Severity: high  
Status: active

Risk:

```text
Conceptual relationships between repositories may be overstated as formal dependencies or proofs.
```

Mitigation:

```text
Use claim-boundary standard and cross-repository dependency classes.
```

---

## RISK-003 — Stale Repository Status

Severity: medium  
Status: active

Risk:

```text
Sub-repository development may advance without Nexus status updates.
```

Mitigation:

```text
Synchronize DEVELOPMENT_STATUS.md with registry/repository_status.md during weekly review.
```

---

## RISK-004 — Publication Before Audit

Severity: high  
Status: active

Risk:

```text
A publication artifact may be shared externally before claim boundaries, limitations, and dependencies are audited.
```

Mitigation:

```text
Require publication checklist and pre-publication audit.
```

---

## Maintenance Rule

Risks should be reviewed during weekly operations.
