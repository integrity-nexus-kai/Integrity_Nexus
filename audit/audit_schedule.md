# Audit Schedule

## Purpose

This document defines the recurring audit schedule for Integrity Nexus and connected repositories.

---

## Audit Cadence

### Weekly Operational Review

Scope:

- active roadmap items,
- open blockers,
- publication status,
- metrics updates.

---

### Monthly Governance Audit

Scope:

- repository status,
- claim boundaries,
- dependency maps,
- maturity model alignment.

---

### Quarterly Repository Audit

Scope:

- all connected repositories,
- required artifacts,
- registry health,
- citation metadata,
- limitations.

---

### Pre-Publication Audit

Scope:

- publication checklist,
- claim classification,
- evidence status,
- open problems,
- limitations.
