# Domain Packs

Domain packs apply the universal TIG-E Foundation Layer to concrete problem spaces.

A domain pack may define domain-specific terminology, candidate types, evidence standards, registries, and open questions.

## Principle

```text
Foundation is universal.
Domain packs are applications.
```

Domain-specific concepts must not be promoted into the Foundation Layer unless they are abstracted into domain-neutral governance rules.

## Initial Domain Packs

Planned or active domain packs may include:

- `physics/` — Integrity Field, field-equation work, physics candidates, physics open questions.
- `pharma/` — molecule/pathway/candidate discovery structures.
- `ai_alignment/` — alignment constraints, governance candidates, safety invariants.
- `psychology/` — deep-structure and behavior-generator analysis.
- `materials/` — material-property search spaces and structural candidates.

This directory is a routing layer, not a final taxonomy.
