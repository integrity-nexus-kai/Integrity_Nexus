# Success Metrics

## Purpose

This document defines measurable success criteria for Integrity Nexus and connected repositories.

---

# Repository Metrics

- 100% of connected repositories have a current status entry.
- 100% of connected repositories have a project card.
- 100% of connected repositories have visible limitations.
- 100% of connected repositories have citation metadata or a citation plan.

---

# Governance Metrics

- 100% of cross-repository claims are classified.
- 100% of maturity changes are recorded.
- 100% of major dependencies are mapped.
- 100% of unresolved audit findings are tracked.

---

# Research Metrics

- All active research tracks link to a roadmap item.
- All critical open problems link to a repository and status.
- All formal claims link to proof obligations or evidence status.

---

# Publication Metrics

- Every publication candidate has a status entry.
- Every publication candidate passes the publication checklist before release.
- Every publication candidate has claim boundaries and limitations reviewed.

---

# Operational Metrics

- Weekly review completed.
- Audit log updated after audits.
- Decision log updated after major governance decisions.
- Risk register updated after major blockers.
