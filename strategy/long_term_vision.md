# Long-Term Vision

## Purpose

This document records the long-term vision of the Integrity Nexus research ecosystem.

---

# Vision Statement

Integrity Nexus aims to become a research operating system for integrity-oriented scientific and technical programs.

It should make complex research architectures navigable, auditable, publishable, and governable.

---

# Long-Term Direction

The ecosystem should support:

- rigorous mathematical research,
- AI-control and systems-safety research,
- recursive governance research,
- cross-repository traceability,
- publication readiness,
- and machine-readable research audit.

---

# Long-Term Target

```text
A coherent multi-repository research program where every claim, dependency, limitation, open problem, and publication path is traceable.
```

---

# Non-Goal

Integrity Nexus should not become an inflated universal theory.

It should remain a disciplined governance and navigation layer for domain-specific research programs.

---

# Strategic Principle

```text
Coherence without collapse.
```

Repositories should remain domain-specific while sharing standards, terminology, and governance discipline.
