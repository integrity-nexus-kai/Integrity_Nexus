# Strategic Objectives

## Purpose

This document defines the top-level strategic objectives for the Integrity Nexus research ecosystem.

---

# SO-001 — Establish SSC Formal Admissibility Framework

Priority: critical  
Repositories: SSC, Integrity Nexus

Objective:

```text
Develop the formal basis for admissible states, admissible transitions, verifier soundness, and enforcement finality.
```

---

# SO-002 — Publish TIG in Peer-Review-Ready Form

Priority: critical  
Repositories: TIG, Integrity Nexus

Objective:

```text
Prepare TIG for scientifically disciplined external review with clear claim boundaries and proof status.
```

---

# SO-003 — Formalize SIR Recursive Governance

Priority: high  
Repositories: SIR, Integrity Nexus

Objective:

```text
Define recursive governance structures that preserve integrity across evolving research programs.
```

---

# SO-004 — Establish Integrity Nexus as Governance System of Record

Priority: high  
Repositories: Integrity Nexus

Objective:

```text
Maintain canonical status, maturity, dependency, audit, publication, and operations layers.
```

---

# SO-005 — Build Machine-Readable Research Architecture

Priority: high  
Repositories: all

Objective:

```text
Make the research ecosystem readable by human reviewers and automated LLM-based audit systems.
```

---

## Maintenance Rule

Every strategic objective must map to roadmap items and measurable success metrics.
